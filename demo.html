The People Behind It

Built by developers who understand real estate lead chaos.

We watched agencies in Pakistan lose qualified buyers every single day — not because of bad agents, but because the follow-up was slow. A lead comes in at midnight, nobody replies until morning. By then, they've already called someone else.

So we built what we wished existed. Owmuq is not a generic chatbot. It is a system built specifically for how Pakistani real estate leads behave — on WhatsApp, at odd hours, in Urdu, Roman Urdu, and English.
